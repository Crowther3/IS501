Product Management

My Career Trajectory

Why MBA?

After 3 years of Project Management I recognized that my career growth plan was limited without having an engineering background.

I recognized in order to pivot out of Product Management and into a better career path a Full-time MBA was the best option available. I spent months preparing for this life altering change and that's when I really learned about what I want to do with the rest of my life.

Learning about Product Management

In preparing for the MBA I did a lot of info interviews to figure out what career paths were out there. It was during this time that I learned about Product Management. I was able to ask many questions and get many resources to help understand what that career was.

I also used lots of resources from the Utah Product Hive Page

Here is a brief video explaining what Product Managers do.

I very quickly recognized my personal skills, as well as my historical experience and successes of working alongside engineers and knew that this was the path I needed to be on.

My Future in Product Management

I am very excited and hopeful to create really innovative products that can actually bless and benefit people's every day lives. I look forward to my first position in a company as a product manager and hope to utilize that as well as others in that company to continue to develop myself into the best product manager I can. From there I will continue growing and look for products and experiences where I can see myself adding the most value as well as finding companies and products with a mission and product that I really believe in.
